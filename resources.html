Resources

Here is a list of places full of useful information for your Divinity Engine 2 needs:

The Divinity Engine 2 Wiki, the first place you should go looking for any information, no matter how specific.

The Larian Studios Discord server, for when you have literally any question about modding. There are loads of helpful people in this server, and they will 99% of the time give you the help you need.

Windemere RPG's YouTube channel, which has some good video tutorials on quest making and story scripting.

Dima 001's YouTube channel, which has some good videos on general engine use and loads of other tutorials.
